# Changelog

## 0.0.1

- Notes sidebar that browses a folder of your choice.
- One-key daily notes (default `Ctrl+Alt+J`) with a customizable template, date format, file extension, and target subfolder.
- Create, rename, delete, and drag-and-drop notes and folders from the sidebar.
- Private and dependency-free: plain local files, no account, no telemetry.
